When adding, moving, or editing notes in the Obsidian vault (`~/Dropbox/Notes/`), follow the conventions documented in `~/Dropbox/Notes/_conventions.md`:
- Filenames: kebab-case, timestamp prefix for leaves, no prefix for structure notes
- Frontmatter: tags (domain + topic + type), aliases, created, lang — required on every note
- Tags: domain matches folder, topic is sub-category, type is one of explanation/how-to/tutorial/reference/cheatsheet/structure-note
- Structure notes: Diataxis sections (Core Concepts, How-To, Cheatsheets & Reference, Related), tagged `structure-note`
- Wiki-links: bare filenames only (`[[name]]`), no paths or `.md`
